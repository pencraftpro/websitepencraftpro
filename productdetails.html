3-in-1 Stylus Pen


₱513.00

The 3-in-1 Stylus Pen functions as a stylus for capacitive touchscreens, a regular ballpoint pen, and a precision tip tool. It is used to interact with touchscreen devices such as the 10.1-inch monitor in digital writing or drawing tasks. The stylus improves accuracy, especially in note-taking or sketching applications, and is ideal for users who require a hybrid input solution. It bridges the gap between traditional handwriting and modern digital interaction.

Raspberry Pi 5 (8GB RAM, 128GB Storage)

₱9,124.00

The Raspberry Pi 5 is a single-board computer designed to deliver powerful processing in a compact form. It is built with a quad-core ARM Cortex-A76 CPU running at 2.4GHz, 4GB or 8GB of LPDDR4X RAM, and improved GPU performance. It offers multiple connectivity options, including dual micro-HDMI outputs, USB 3.0 ports, Gigabit Ethernet, Bluetooth 5.0, and Wi-Fi 802.11ac. The Pi 5 also features a 40-pin GPIO header for hardware interfacing and expansion. It is widely used for embedded applications, educational purposes, and IoT systems due to its affordability, flexibility, and open-source community support.

10.1-inch Touchscreen Monitor

₱5,704.00

The 10.1-inch Touchscreen Monitor is a high-resolution display component with capacitive touch support. It offers a wide viewing angle, USB and HDMI interfaces for power and video input, and plug-and-play compatibility with the Raspberry Pi. The screen supports multi-touch gestures like tapping, pinching, and swiping, making it ideal for GUI-based systems. It provides a more intuitive user experience in interactive projects and embedded systems.
