Hardware Guide

Detailed instructions about the hardware requirements and setup for PenCraft Pro.

Hardware Requirements

Ensure your device meets the minimum hardware requirements for PenCraft Pro to run smoothly.

  • Mobile (Android): Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later with at least 2GB of RAM and 100MB of free storage space.
  • Compatible Accessories: Stylus pen, touchscreen monitors, and external storage devices.

Hardware Setup

Follow these steps to set up and connect your hardware with PenCraft Pro.

  • Using your stylus pen: The stylus pen works passively and does not require charging, batteries, or pairing. Simply start using it on supported touchscreen devices for writing or drawing.
  • Setting up a touchscreen monitor: Connect your touchscreen monitor using an HDMI or USB-C cable. Calibrate the screen for accurate touch response.
  • Installing the APK:
    1. Download the APK file from the Download Page.
    2. Open the APK file on your Android device.
    3. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" if prompted.
    4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues with your hardware, try the following solutions.

  • Stylus not working: Ensure the stylus is compatible with your device and the touchscreen is functioning correctly. Check for driver updates or calibration issues.
  • Monitor not displaying: Ensure all cables are securely connected. Check the input source on your monitor.
  • APK installation issues: Ensure "Install from Unknown Sources" is enabled in your Android device settings. Alternatively, check if the APK file is corrupted and re-download it.

Maintenance Tips

Keep your hardware in optimal condition by following these maintenance tips.

  • Regular cleaning: Use a microfiber cloth to clean screens and device surfaces. Avoid using abrasive cleaners.
  • Stylus care: Handle your stylus with care to avoid damaging the tip or scratching the screen.
  • Storage and handling: Store hardware in a cool, dry place when not in use. Avoid exposing devices to extreme temperatures.
